My journey into the world of coding commenced around 2008, during my second year at university. Back then, my maiden project was a Patient Management System for a hospital using Microsoft Visual Basics (front-end) and Microsoft Access (back-end), and from that moment onward, I was captivated.

I was truly fascinated by not only the intricate logic behind crafting applications but also by the art of making them visually appealing to users given my keen eye for detail. Those two laid the foundation for my foray into the realm of full-stack development, igniting my career.

My quest for knowledge is unceasing, as I continually explore new technologies and concepts to stay abreast of the ever-evolving trends of our digital age. Sharing my insights through written articles is my way of giving back to the community. I find joy in crafting imaginative designs on Codepen and indulging in the world of graphic design, cherishing the beauty of aesthetics.

Currently, my focus lies on advancing to the next stage of my career, striving to attain the status of a mid-level developer.

Thank you.
